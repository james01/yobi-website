Max is the Founder and CEO of Yobi. Since 2014, he has been at the intersection of data, media, and technology working to help enterprises better manage, activate, and improve their digital strategies. Prior to Yobi, Max founded a data measurement company that provided critical research in over $500M of investments and acquisitions across the Entertainment and Advertising industries. He also founded a digital music distribution company that leveraged data to drive smarter and cheaper digital marketing campaigns resulting in nearly 1 billion monetized streams/views. Max attended Duke University.
